A simple and delicious butter cookie made in 30 minutes !

 These butter cookies give youa rich taste and soft textur the moment you eat them.
That gives you comfort. It's easy and simple to make, so you don't have to buy it with money.


Butter cookies are literally cookies made with a lot of butter.
It is a dessert that anyone can make because it is simple to make.

Ingredients

1. Butter 150g
2. Soft flour 200g
3. Sugar 100g
4. An egg

Direction

1. Put the butter in a bowl.


2. Mash the butter.


3. Put the sugar.


4. Mix until the sound is reduced.


5. When the noise is reduced, add the egg


6. Mix


7. If you're done mixing it, put in the flour.


8. Mix


9. Then it becomes a heavy dough.


10. Put it in a piping bag.


11.After squeezing it nicely, bake the dough in an oven preheated at 180 degrees.


12. Complete!
